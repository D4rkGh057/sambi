About Us

At Sambi we create and enjoy high-quality artisanal chocolate, with the best ingredients and traditional techniques, sharing our passion for chocolate in every bite.

Services

What we do.

At SAMBI, we create experiences through artisanal chocolate. Born from a university dream, we perfect our recipe to offer a product of excellence. Every bite is a connection with our customers and a tradition full of passion. Discover the magic of SAMBI and enjoy a little piece of Ambato in every bite.

Premium Ingredient Selection

SAMBI is distinguished by its careful selection of premium ingredients, ensuring that each chocolate is made with the best ingredients available to guarantee an exceptional taste experience.

Unparalleled Flavor

SAMBI offers chocolates with an authentic and delicious taste that delights the most demanding palates, providing unparalleled sensory experiences that satisfy the senses and leave a lasting impression.

Personalized Happiness Experience

SAMBI goes beyond simply offering chocolates; it's about creating memorable and meaningful moments for our customers. From customizing unique gifts to meticulous attention to every detail, we are dedicated to conveying joy and happiness through our delicious chocolates.

Personalized Customer Service

SAMBI provides close and attentive customer service, offering personalized product recommendations, helping customers find the perfect chocolate for every occasion and ensuring a friendly and welcoming shopping experience both in-store and online.
